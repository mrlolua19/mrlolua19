Maple Syrup with Tuna Fish

This unexpected combination of maple syrup and tuna creates a unique and savory dish that will surprise and delight your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • Tuna fillets
  • Maple syrup
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate tuna fillets in maple syrup and seasonings.
  2. Grill the fish.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy!

Maple Syrup Popsicle

Taste the amazing, cool Canadian food puting in minimum effort.

Ingredients:

  • Maple syrup
  • Snow
  • Stick

Instructions:

  1. Pour the syrup on the snow.
  2. Wait till the puddle freezes.
  3. Put a stick at the start or at the end of the frozen puddle.
  4. Roll up the puddle around the stick.
  5. Enjoy your homemade maple syrup popsicles!

Maple Syrup Popcorn

Add a sweet twist to your favorite snack with maple syrup popcorn.

Ingredients:

  • Popcorn kernels
  • Maple syrup
  • Butter or coconut oil
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Pop the popcorn kernels using your preferred method.
  2. In a saucepan, heat maple syrup and butter or coconut oil until melted and combined.
  3. Drizzle the maple syrup mixture over the popcorn and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Sprinkle with salt to taste.
  5. Spread the popcorn on a baking sheet and bake at 120°C for 15–25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until crisp.
  6. Let cool before serving and enjoy your delicious maple syrup popcorn!
